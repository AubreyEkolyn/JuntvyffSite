Scalebeasts

Roaming not just the Ancient Field, but Limeth and the Highlands too, are the scalebeasts. Ranging from the size of a dog to towering over the treetops, scalebeasts are creatures of various forms covered in scales, but unlike reptiles are also covered in fur or feathers. One such example is the fearsome "orc hunter" of the Highlands, a massive cat-like creature with a taste for orcs.

Zephrite-infused stones

Throughout the Ancient Field are patches of floating rock, usually surrounding a hedron. The source of both's mysterious floating is zephrite, a strange mineral that resists gravity while affected by strong energy. Crystals of zephrite large enough to be of any use (to non-stoneweavers) are rare in the Ancient Field, which limits the size of these stones -- unlike the shattered peaks of Twyrlode. These "stoneclouds" can become dangerous if excited by wild magic, becoming whirling storms of stone shards that can tear creatures to bits.

A more peaceful location of rocks infused with zephrite exists in the AF, known as the ruined spires. Here, pillars of stone that were once whole float immobile with enough space between the sections for the average person to walk through.

Hedrons

Hedrons dot the fields, ancient stone prisms with mazes carved into their sides. Whatever purpose they were created for seems to have been served, as many lay dormant, buried, or crumbling. Some are still active, carried aloft by drifts of power, surrounded by their own broken pieces and other zephrite-infused stones they pick up as they float around. Others are buried but active, creating clouds of floating stone above their location.

The Sand Sea

The largest continent in Juntvyyf is dominated by a giant desert. Creatures here swim through the sand like water, and other inhabitants navigate the treacherous sands in sandships borne by the scorching winds.

The Giant's field

At the end of the southern arm of Epheira rests Twyrlode, the highest mountain range in Juntvyyf. Home of the Goliath race and many giantkin, the giant's field is a high-altitude plain nestled into the range. The very tip of the arm is the world's crown and where the storm obelisk rests.
