Hello!

This is a home page that introduces the viewer (you) to the photographer (me)!

I call myself a photographer, but that really is not true. I know very little about taking photos and the techniques used for taking good ones, though that does not stop me from sometimes taking a picture of scenery that I like or that provides me with ideas for writing. I always take photos with my phone, so I did some digging through it for this website. I pulled out a selection of some of the photos I have taken for your enjoyment! Just click on the Portfolio tab, and leave a review on the Review tab!
